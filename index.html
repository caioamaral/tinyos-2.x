Warning!

This repository is not ready, and there is not much to see right now, but I have been working to bring you a nice tutorial on how to port the TinyOS on you custom made platform.

Looking for how to install TinyOS on your custom MSP430 platform?

Here I will show you how to port the broadly used Wireless sensor network operating system to your custom platform composed by MSP430 and a radio. In my case MSP430F5510 and CC2520.
